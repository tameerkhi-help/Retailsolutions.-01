HOW WE WORK

Onboarding is Seamless.

We integrate with your store in 3 simple steps without disrupting your day-to-day business.

1

Phase 1: Tech Audit & Consultation

We review your current POS, CCTV systems, and back-office software to identify margin leaks and inefficiencies. We configure your existing tools for better performance.

2

Phase 2: System Integration & Team Coverage

We securely connect our remote team to your existing platforms. No new software required. We train continuously on every platform update so you get flawless, up-to-date management.

3

Phase 3: Daily Human-Operated Management

Your dedicated remote team takes over daily pricing updates, surveillance review, inventory monitoring, and back-office reconciliation. You get weekly reports and total financial clarity.
